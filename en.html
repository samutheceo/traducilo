Who we are?

About us

Traducilo.com is the ideal translation agency for companies that need to communicate or conduct business in Latin America effortlessly, using native English, Portuguese or Spanish. Speaking the same language as your clients is an effective strategy to reach a wider audience, build lasting relationships and convey a more positive image of your brand. When it comes to translation, Traducilo.com is the strategic partner you have been looking for. This women-owned boutique translation agency was founded in 2004 by Carina Franzoni and Gabriela Geminiani after their previous translation project known as Nexo – Translation Services. They became friends while working as in-house translators at a large Japanese company and decided to pursue their dream as entrepreneurs and linguists.

Satisfied customers say they are detail-oriented, able to work under pressure and can always meet tight deadlines, while fostering a team environment.
